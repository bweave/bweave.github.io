About

Hi there! I'm Brian.

I build stuff on the internet, play drums, and brew beer.

I've been working on the web for roughly 10 years. I got my start doing freelance WordPress work. Didn't we all? Ha! For the past 6 years, I've been doing full stack development: everything from server setup to CSS. I really love being involved in the full lifecycle of a product or site.

On the backend, I have extensive experience in PHP land with many production sites built on WordPress, CodeIgniter, & Laravel. I also have experience architecting and scaling the LAMP/LEMP stack to serve 10s to 100s of thousands of users. In the last 2 years, I've done a good bit of work with Ruby on Rails and Postgres. LOVE IT! At this point, I'd be happy to work in PHP provided it were a Larvel project. However, given the choice, I'd choose Rails.

On the frontend, I have extensive experience with Bootstrap, Foundation, & jQuery. I've built single page apps with Angular, & Backbone, and hybrid mobile apps with Ionic.

I prefer Ansible for sysadmin/devops duties. I love its power and simplicity! Whenever possible though, I'm a huge fan of partnering vs. pioneering infrastructure and my partner of choice is Heroku. Let the fantastic engineers at Heroku do what they do best, so that I can focus on providing clients and users with value.

Other stuff that I've tooled around with include: MongoDB, EmberJS, NodeJS & Express, Chef.
